Bulacan Local Government Unit

The local government unit (LGU) of Bulacan is responsible for the governance and administration of the province. It is headed by a governor who is elected by the people for a term of three years. The LGU is composed of various departments and offices that oversee different aspects of governance such as finance, health, education, tourism, and public works.
